About Us

SGT PEB Infrastructure & Building Solution Pvt Ltd

SGT PEB Infrastructure & Building Solution Pvt Ltd is a leading pre-engineered building (PEB) construction company based in Jalna, Maharashtra. With a proven track record of delivering high-quality warehouses, industrial structures, and commercial buildings, SGT has earned the trust of clients across the region through its commitment to precision, durability, and professionalism. Our team of skilled engineers and construction experts ensures every project is completed on time, within budget, and to the highest standards.

Located in Krishna Arcade, Jalna, we have successfully completed multiple large-scale warehouse and industrial projects at Jalna MIDC and beyond. Our client-first approach, transparent communication, and attention to detail set us apart in the PEB sector. Whether you are looking to build a new warehouse, factory shed, or commercial complex, SGT PEB Infrastructure is your trusted partner from concept to completion.
